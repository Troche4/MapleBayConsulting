Who We Are

Maple Bay Consulting is led by its president, Joe Walker. Throughout his business career, Joe has lead international companies, a professional services firm, as well as for-profit and not-for-profit associations and foundations. Joe has served as a:

Board Member
CEO
COO
General Counsel
President of an International Division
Consultant

Joe received the Vistage Soaring Eagle Award, the Providence Foundation Daughters of Charity Order of Charity Award and the Lifetime Achievement Award from Life Directions Inc.
He has also served as a member of the Michigan State Board of Ethics and the State Bar of Michigan Professional Ethics Committee.

Joe is a frequent lecturer on the subjects of Strategic Planning, Leadership and Leadership Development, Team-Building, Quality, Ethics, and Law Firm Management.
He is co-author of two books: Leading a Motown Turnaround: How a 130-year-old Company Went from Team to Tactics to Become New Again (2005) and TQM in Action: One Firm’s Journey toward Quality and Excellence (1994), published by the American Bar Association.

Our Process

Business transformation, transition or turnaround can be an evolution or a revolution. It doesn’t matter if it is a course correction or a 180 degree turnaround, the challenge is still the same. It’s about leading change. It could be an acquisition, a divestiture, a need for a new direction or the need for a new company culture. The only solution we have found that leads to success is speed, discipline, and focus and accountability around change. In a word, it is leadership. We lead change by taking organizations from transformation to triumph.
