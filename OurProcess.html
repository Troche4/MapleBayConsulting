Financial Turnaround

For a Business Turnaround, the model is to lead companies that are in financial crisis to a profitable situation as soon as possible. You need a leader and a team. We address the people, process and technology challenges to get you to stability, then profitability. Our experience covers both domestic and international businesses.

Accomplishing a Business Turnaround requires immediate and rigorous financial analysis (we follow the money). Next, we carefully select a team; we address the short term people, process and technology issues to create a plan. To implement the plan and be successful, you need a disciplined and focused leader, an aligned and dedicated team that will stand up to the challenges.
